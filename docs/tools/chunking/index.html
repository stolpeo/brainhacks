Chunking

Chunks are compact packages of information that are easy accessible for the mind. They are part of the bigger picture that you want to form, but easier to grasp for the focused mind. Chunking is the technique to form chunks and to connect them.

How-To

  1. Break your big picture down into managable tasks. It is important to understand the big picture!
  2. Alternate between the following two:
    • Practice these tasks in focused mode using pomodoro technique and spaced repetition.
    • Use diffuse mode and sleep to connect chunks. Have the big picture in mind!
